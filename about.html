Our Story

Luminous_Photography was founded by Thami Mosehlana, a passionate visual storyteller dedicated to capturing the beauty of every moment. His journey began with a deep love for art and creativity, evolving into a professional pursuit of photography that celebrates emotion, light, and authenticity. The company was built on a vision to tell meaningful stories through the lens — with excellence, integrity, and purpose at its core.

At Luminous_Photography, faith and passion guide every frame we capture. Each photograph is crafted with care and precision — not just to document moments, but to preserve memories that inspire and endure. Over the years, Thami has collaborated with individuals, couples, and brands across Johannesburg and beyond, offering a blend of artistry and professionalism that brings every vision to life.

We believe photography is more than images — it’s emotion, connection, and storytelling combined. We shoot to celebrate life, highlight beauty, and share stories that matter. By working closely with clients, we ensure every session reflects their unique identity and purpose. Whether it’s a portrait, event, or brand shoot, Luminous_Photography approaches every project with grace, creativity, and meticulous attention to detail.
